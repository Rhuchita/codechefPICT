About Us

PICT Student Chapter is a programming club run and maintained by official Chapter leaders and mentored by Codechef. We bring a competitive programming culture on the campus under the mentorship of CodeChef. This enables students to learn together with other colleges in the Codechef Community. We provide opportunities and exposure to students on the campus.
